1. General Overview

> Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.
> Known as the "Blue Planet" due to its abundance of water.
> Diameter: ~12,742 km (largest of the terrestrial planets).

2. Orbit and Rotation

> Earth takes ~365.25 days to complete one orbit around the Sun.
> A day (rotation period) lasts ~24 hours.
> Axis tilt of 23.5° causes the seasons.

3. Surface and Composition

> Surface is 71% water and 29% land, with diverse terrains like mountains, plains, and oceans.
> Divided into tectonic plates that move and cause earthquakes.
> Composed of a crust, mantle, outer core, and inner core (mainly iron and nickel).

4. Atmosphere and Temperature

> Atmosphere is 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace gases like carbon dioxide.
> Average surface temperature: ~15°C (59°F).
> Protective ozone layer shields the planet from harmful solar radiation.

5. Unique Facts

> Earth is the only planet with liquid water on its surface.
> Has one natural satellite: the Moon.
> Supports a wide range of ecosystems and diverse life forms.
