1. General Overview

> Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun.
> Named after the Roman messenger god due to its swift orbit.
> Diameter: ~4,880 km (about 38% the size of Earth).

2. Orbit and Rotation

> Mercury has the shortest orbital period: ~88 Earth days.
> Day length (solar day): ~176 Earth days.
> Spins slowly, completing a rotation every 59 Earth days.

3. Surface and Composition

> Surface resembles the Moon: covered in craters and smooth plains.
> Key features: Caloris Basin, one of the largest impact craters in the solar system.
> Composed mainly of rock and metal, with a large iron core (~85% of the planet's radius).

4. Atmosphere and Temperature

Unique Facts

> Mercury has no moons or rings.
> Its magnetic field is 1% as strong as Earth's but still significant for its size.
> The planet has ice at its poles, shielded in permanently shadowed craters.
