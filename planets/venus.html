1. General Overview

> Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's "sister planet."
> Named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.
> Diameter: ~12,104 km (about 95% the size of Earth).

2. Orbit and Rotation

> Venus has an orbital period of ~225 Earth days.
> A day on Venus (rotation period) lasts ~243 Earth days, longer than its year.
> Rotates in the opposite direction (retrograde rotation) compared to most planets.

3. Surface and Composition

> Surface is covered with volcanic plains, mountains, and vast lava flows.
> Features: Maxwell Montes (highest peak) and Ishtar Terra (a highland region).
> Composed mainly of silicate rocks with a metallic iron-nickel core.

4. Atmosphere and Temperature

> Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere made mostly of carbon dioxide with clouds of sulfuric acid.
> Surface temperature averages ~465°C (869°F) due to a runaway greenhouse effect.
> Atmospheric pressure is ~92 times that of Earth's.

5. Unique Facts

> Venus has no moons or rings.
> Brightest object in the night sky after the Moon.
> Known for its "retrograde rotation," causing the Sun to rise in the west and set in the east.
